Div Yield ?
—
Annual dividend as % of price
Dividend Yield = Annual Dividend ÷ Stock Price. >1% is considered decent for Indian stocks. High yield can mean good income, but verify it's sustainable.
Trail EPS ?
—
Earnings per share (last 12m)
Trailing EPS = Net Profit ÷ Total Shares (last 4 quarters). Higher EPS = more profitable. Growing EPS year-over-year is a bullish signal.
ROE ?
—
Return on shareholders' equity
Return on Equity = Net Income ÷ Shareholders' Equity × 100. >15% is considered good. Shows how efficiently management generates profit.
Debt/Eq ?
—
Financial leverage risk
Debt-to-Equity = Total Debt ÷ Total Equity. Lower is safer. <1x is ideal for non-banks. High D/E = more financial risk during downturns.
P/B Ratio ?
—
Price vs. book value of assets
Price-to-Book = Stock Price ÷ Book Value Per Share. <3x is reasonable. >6x can indicate an overvalued stock. Best compared within same sector.

🚀 The MarketPulse Journey

MarketPulse represents the convergence of advanced financial analytics, real-time data processing, and scalable backend architecture.

The core engineering challenge was delivering a comprehensive, enterprise-grade intelligence platform—featuring real-time market screening, a seamless journaling TradeDesk, and the AI-driven InvestCircle engine—within the strict memory limits of a cloud Free Tier on Render.

To overcome these constraints, I architected a custom Python and SQLite backend optimized for extreme efficiency. By implementing intelligent lazy-loading strategies for heavy machine learning models, specifically ChromaDB and Sentence-Transformers, I successfully bypassed the environment's memory ceilings. This meticulous resource management ensures zero-lag performance while maintaining sophisticated AI capabilities.

The frontend is a pure, dependency-free implementation utilizing modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It features a responsive, glassmorphic design system that provides a fluid and visually stunning user experience without the overhead of heavy frameworks. MarketPulse stands as a testament to the fact that with relentless optimization and visionary engineering, uncompromising performance is always achievable.
